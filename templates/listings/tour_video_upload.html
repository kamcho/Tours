Quick Tips for Great Tour Videos

Make your video stand out and attract more travelers

Start Strong

Begin with your most exciting footage to grab attention in the first 10 seconds.

Show Real Experiences

Include actual travelers enjoying activities, not just scenic shots.

Keep it Concise

2-5 minutes is perfect. Longer videos often lose viewer engagement.

Add Text Overlays

Include key information like tour highlights, prices, or contact details.

End with Action

Close with a clear call-to-action encouraging viewers to book the tour.

Quality Matters

Use stable shots, good lighting, and clear audio for professional results.
